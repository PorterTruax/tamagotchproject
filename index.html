Tam-a-gatchi

Long ago, humanity grew bored with itself.

Hungry for meaning and fatigued from procreation, scientists created a new race: Tamagatchis.

You have been entrusted with one of these precious beings. And like any living thing, it has needs.

Feed it.

Give it rest.

Give it life.

Age: Tamagatchi hasn't been born yet!

Care for your tama! If its sleepiness, boredom, or hunger become greater than 10, it will have a breakdown! But be careful. Mess with your Tama too much and it might runaway...
